Next Chapter: Book Sharing Platform

Problem Description

"Next Chapter" is a web app aimed at tackling the problem of book accessibility and overconsumption in reading. The core idea is to create a platform where people can swap or give away books fostering a community-driven approach. This concept addresses several key issues: the inaccessibility of books for certain demographics, and the environmental impact of overconsumption.

Unlike many existing platforms, "Next Chapter" would be a place to swap and give them away. This creates a more sustainable lifecycle for books. The platform would include features like a virtual bookshelf for users to showcase their collection, and a search and filter system to find specific books.

Proposed Application

Existing solutions and their limitations

  • Some platforms like Goodreads primarily focus on book tracking and recommendations, not on the physical exchange of books.
  • Sites like BookMooch or PaperBackSwap do exist for book swapping, but they often lack a robust community interaction feature and a comprehensive system for libraries.

App Description

Users of the Application

The primary users of this application are those who love books and are seeking sustainable ways to access a wide range of them. This may include individuals who are book lovers interested in discovering new books, book collectors looking for new genres, series, and unique books, and individuals who want to start reading to enhance their literacy. Our application is expected to have a positive impact on minority groups. We hope that with a diverse user base, there will be a broad range of literature reflecting different authors, genres, and perspectives. Additionally, we aim to include accessibility features to ensure that our users have a positive experience navigating the app. Overall, our app aims to foster a sense of community among all types of users.
